Microcontrollers & Sensors Course

Course Overview

This course introduces participants to the world of microcontrollers and sensors. It covers the fundamental principles of microcontroller operation, interfacing techniques, and practical applications in various fields.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Introduction to Microcontrollers
  • Basic Electronics and Components
  • Programming Microcontrollers (Arduino)
  • Interfacing Sensors and Actuators
  • Real-world Applications and Projects

Learning Outcomes:

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Understand the architecture of microcontrollers.
  • Write basic programs for microcontroller applications.
  • Interface sensors and actuators to perform various tasks.
  • Implement projects using microcontrollers and sensors.

Certification

Participants will receive a certificate of completion upon finishing the course.

Who Should Enroll?

This course is suitable for:

  • Engineering students and hobbyists interested in electronics.
  • Anyone looking to build projects using microcontrollers.
  • Professionals wanting to understand embedded systems.
